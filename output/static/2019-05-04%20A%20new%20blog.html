I've had quite a few blogs in my life. Most of them still exist in one way or another. I have at least three of them as private blogs in my Blogspot account, none of them with less than three years worth(less) of content. I even had a couple blogs in LiveJournal and another one in CemZoo1. I used to ve an avid blogger, even if not a very good one. Reading them today is cringy to say the least.

My objective with this blog is not to cringe when I read it ten or fifteen or twenty years from now. Which is going to be difficult considering that I'm cringing just by rereading this work-in-progress post.
